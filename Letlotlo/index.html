Got questions?

Are there any additional costs besides the tuition fee?

We keep our pricing transparent. In most cases, there are no additional fees beyond the tuition cost. If optional materials, books, or specialized exam prep resources are recommended, we will always inform you in advance—so there are never any surprise charges.

What subjects do you offer tutoring in?

We offer tutoring in a wide range of subjects, including Maths, English, Science, Accounting, and specialized courses such as Coding and Robotics. We also provide support for standardized test preparation.

What grade levels do you serve?

We work with students from primary school through high school, as well as  learners seeking academic support.

How does the tutoring process work?

We begin with an initial assessment to understand the student’s strengths, challenges, and goals. Based on the results, we create a personalized learning plan and begin sessions with a qualified tutor.

How do I sign up for tutoring sessions?

You can sign up through our online registration form or by contacting our office directly. Our team will help match you with a tutor and schedule your first session.
