About Us

"A culture-driven fashion house curating official artist merchandise and handcrafted KasiXpage denim."

KasiXpage Fashion House (Pty) Ltd is a streetwear brand founded by visionary creative Dennis Nyathi in Soweto, the brand champions township culture by blending handcrafted African expression with global fashion influence.What began with 20 hoodies sod out, yet mishandled has evolved into a redemption journey stitched into every piece.Known as "Africa's loudest whisper",we are a bold, township-born fashion house fusing African heritage with cutting-edge streetwear.Kasixpage is more than fashion, it's a cultural movement rooted in honesty,resilience,and reinvention.

We use fashion to tell stories of struggle, triumph, and bold authenticity. Our patchwork denim isn't just design, it's declaration.Every stitch is a reminder that real style comes from lived experience, not trends.

Dennis Nyathi, Founder and Visionary
